Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious and Soft Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe was forged in the fires of Hel when the Great Odin was struck by a lust for chocolatey goodness. Really simple and easy to make it's pretty awesome actually.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 (1/8 if using salted butter) teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with the salt.
  5. Stir in the flour and the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  7. Bake for about 12 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
